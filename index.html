NeuroPipe by ntblab

NeuroPipe is a framework for reproducible fMRI analysis projects with FSL, developed in the Turk-Browne Lab at Princeton University.

Getting Started

  1. Join the mailing list. Email it with any problems or suggestions.
  2. Work through NeuroPipe's tutorial.
  3. Adapt the tutorial's methods to suit your project. If your analysis involves something the tutorial doesn't cover, ask the mailing list. If you figure it out yourself, tell the mailing list what you did.

Contributing

If you'd like to help out, first be sure you're on the mailing list, then head over to the development wiki.
